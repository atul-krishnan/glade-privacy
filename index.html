Glade — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

Glade (“the app”) is a digital-wellbeing app that helps you pause before opening distracting apps. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Glade does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no servers. Everything the app does happens entirely on your device.

Information Glade accesses

To do its job, Glade uses two Android permissions:

  • Accessibility service — Glade is notified when an app moves to the foreground so it can show a pause screen for the apps you chose to limit. Glade only reads the package name of the app in the foreground. It does not read the contents of your screen, your messages, what you type, your passwords, or any data inside other apps.
  • Display over other apps — lets Glade show its pause screen on top of the app you opened.

The list of apps you choose to limit, and your pause settings, are stored only on your device (in the app’s local storage). This information is never sent anywhere and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Information Glade collects and shares

  • Collected: none.
  • Shared with third parties: none.
  • Sold: never.

Because no data leaves your device, there is nothing for us to access, retain, or disclose.

Children

Glade does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.
